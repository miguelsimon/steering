Overview

Source for building this site can be found at https://github.com/miguelsimon/steering.

This is mainly an exercise to teach myself cmake and C++.

Rapidly exploring random trees

Rapidly exploring random trees are one of the coolest ideas ever, the paper by LaValle is excellent. They allow the statement and solution of extremely broad classes of planning problems.

  • demo_trivial_rrt.html Builds a trivial RRT in 2 dimensions.

Steering behaviours

A few steering behaviours implemented in C++ and compiled to WebAssembly.

  • demo_seek.html Seek steering behaviour, note oscillations when goal is reached.
  • demo_arrive.html Arrive behaviour, no oscillations :)
  • demo_waypoint.html Simple waypoint following.
  • demo_wander.html Wander steering behaviour.
